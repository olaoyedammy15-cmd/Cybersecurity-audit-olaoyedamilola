## Finding report
Target Audited:Personal smartphone(IOS)
Summary
I carried out a basic cybersecurity audit on my personal iOS smartphone to identify common security weaknesses
Findings
	1.	The iPhone has a strong passcode/Face ID enabled.
	2.	iOS is fully updated to the latest version. 
	3.	Some unused applications are still installed.
	4.	App permissions have not been reviewed, some apps may have access to camera, microphone, and location unnecessarily.
	5.Two-factor authentication (2FA) is enabled on the Apple ID.
	7.	Bluetooth and Wi-Fi are left on and connecting automatically to unknown networks.
     Risk Level:Medium Risk
     Why?
The device has strong protections including a passcode, Face ID, and a fully updated operating system. 
However, unreviewed app permissions, weak account security practices, and automatic connections to unknown networks can still expose 
personal data to attackers.
